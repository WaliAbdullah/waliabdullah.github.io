Welcome!

I am currently an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics and Information Technology at Concordia University of Edmonton. My academic journey includes a Ph.D. in Theoretical & Computational Science from the University of Lethbridge, Canada, where I was part of the Optimization Research Group under the supervision of Dr. Shahadat Hossain. Prior to that, I earned a Master's in Information and Communication Technology from the IICT at the Bangladesh University of Engineering Technology (BUET), following my graduation from the Military Institute of Science and Technology (MIST).

In addition to my academic roles, I worked as a software engineering researcher at Huawei Technologies Canada for nearly two years. As an educator, I have served as a lecturer at the University of Lethbridge and the Military Institute of Science and Technology. I believe that collaboration between academia and industry is crucial for technological innovation, and I am dedicated to advancing my research in the IT sector.

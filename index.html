Welcome!

I am a Software Engineering Researcher at Huawei Technologies Canada Co Ltd.

My academic background includes a Ph.D. in Theoretical & Computer Science from the University of Lethbridge, Canada, where I was a part of the Optimization Research Group under the guidance of Dr. Shahadat Hossain. Prior to that, I obtained a Master's in Information and Communication Technology from the IICT of the Bangladesh University of Engineering Technology after graduating from the Military Institute of Science and Technology.

As an educator, I have previously served as a lecturer at both the University of Lethbridge and the Military Institute of Science and Technology. I strongly believe that academia and industry play critical roles in driving technological progress, and I am committed to continuing my research efforts to contribute to the advancement of the IT sector.
